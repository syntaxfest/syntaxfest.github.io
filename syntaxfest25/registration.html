Registration includes access to all five workshops under a single fee:

  • IWPT: 18th International Conference on Parsing Technologies (August 26)
  • UDW: 8th Universal Dependencies Workshop (August 27)
  • DepLing: 8th International Conference on Dependency Linguistics (August 27-28)
  • TLT: 23rd Workshop on Treebanks and Linguistic Theories (August 28-29)
  • QUASY: 3rd Workshop on Quantitative Syntax (August 29)

The registration fee includes access to the full conference program, covering all sessions from Tuesday to Friday, with morning and afternoon coffee breaks, cafeteria lunches, a welcome reception, and a guided tour of Ljubljana. All prices include 22% VAT.

Conference Dinner

To keep the conference program accessible, especially for students and daily participants, the conference dinner, taking place on Thursday evening (August 28), is offered as an optional extra (50 EUR). Additional tickets can also be purchased for accompanying persons.

Co-located Events

Admission to the UniDive Shared Task Workshop on Morphosyntactic Parsing (MSP) and the UniDive UD Turkic workshop is free of charge, but pre-registration is required for both:

  • To register for the UniDive MSP Shared Task workshop, please use the main SyntaxFest registration form.
  • To register for the UD Turkic Workshop, please visit the workshop page.

Please note that both workshops take place in parallel on Tuesday morning (26 August) and provide access only to the workshop session and coffee break.
