Schedule at a Glance

SyntaxFest 2025 is a 3.5-day event, running from Tuesday afternoon, August 26, to Friday, August 29, 2025. On Tuesday morning, we kick off with two pre-conference workshops organized by UniDive COST Action: the UniDive Shared Task on Morphosyntactic Parsing (MSP) and the Workshop on Universal Dependencies for Turkic Languages (UDTW), which will run in parallel.
