Lunch

Free lunch at the cafeteria

Free lunch will be provided for all conference participants from Tuesday to Friday, including attendees of the UniDive workshop. Lunch will take place in the cafeteria on the ground floor of the conference venue and will feature a selection of several daily menus (soup + main dish + salad), including vegetarian options.

Lunch coupons will be distributed at registration and can be used any time during cafeteria opening hours (Tuesday to Thursday from 10:30 to 14:30 and Friday from 10:30 to 14:00). There is also a cafe that is open from 7:00 to 15:00 (7:00 to 14:00 on Friday) where you can get additional coffee.

The menus for each day are displayed below. Vegetarian options are displayed in green. For participants with vegan menus, vegan options can also be prepared.
