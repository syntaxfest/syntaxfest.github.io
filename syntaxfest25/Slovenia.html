Slovenia

From the towering mountaintops of the Alps to the charming blue vistas of its coastline, Slovenia offers a diverse assortment of landscapes to explore. A wide selection of natural, cultural and culinary experiences can be found in virtually every corner of this charismatic country. Travel around the country is very convenient, as most destinations can be reached within a short trip by car, bus, or train from the capital, Ljubljana. For more information on Slovenia, please see Slovenia's official tourist information webpage

Whether you prefer an active hiking or kayaking trip in the Soča River Valley or a picturesque boat tour to the small lake island on Lake Bled, Slovenia has something to offer for everyone. Discover Slovenia's scenic coastline with beautiful seaside towns such as Piran or venture underground and witness the stunning natural wonders of Postojna Cave.
