Instructions for Authors

Oral Presentations

Oral presentations will take place at the conference venue. Each paper is scheduled as either a short talk or a long talk, with the following time limits:

  • Short talks (15 min): 10 minutes for presentation + 5 minutes for Q&A
  • Long talks (20 min): 15 minutes for presentation + 5 minutes for Q&A

Please keep to your allocated time to ensure the smooth flow of the programme.

Presentation rooms are equipped with a PC (Windows) connected to a projector. We recommend that you bring your slides on a USB stick (PowerPoint and PDF formats supported) to ensure a smooth transition between presentations. In case you specifically require to use your own laptop, it is also possible to connect via HDMI.

WiFi is available via Eduroam or designated network.
We recommend checking your setup during the break before your session to avoid any technical issues.

Poster Presentations

Each poster session will begin with a round of lightning talks, where each presenter gives a 2-minute oral introduction to their poster. Presenters may optionally prepare a single slide to accompany their lightning talk. Instructions for uploading this slide via OpenReview will be sent by email closer to the conference.
