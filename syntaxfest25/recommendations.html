Local Recommendations

Ljubljana and Slovenia have a lot to offer. We recommend that you first stop by the Tourist Information Center (Adamič-Lundrovo nabrežje 2), where they will be happy to give you advice and itineraries tailored to your needs. Nevertheless, please find some of our own recommendations below:

Must-See Highlights

  • Walk through the city center: Explore the bridges (Triple Bridge, Dragon Bridge, Cobbler’s Bridge), the main squares (Prešernov trg, Mestni trg, Kongresni trg), and the charming streets along the Ljubljanica River.
  • Ljubljana Central Market: Located just across from the conference venue, this lively morning market offers fresh local produce daily. Don’t miss the covered parts for cheese, charcuterie, fish, and pastries.
  • Ljubljana Castle: We’ll have our conference dinner there, but it’s worth a visit anytime for the panoramic views, exhibitions, and history. You can walk up one of several scenic paths or take the funicular (details here).

Cafés

Ljubljana has a vibrant specialty coffee scene. A few favorites near the venue:

  • Petkovškovo nabrežje (map): A lively riverside promenade lined with cafés and bars, perfect for enjoying a drink with views of the Ljubljanica River.
  • Kavarna ROG (map): Stylish café with a terrace on the first floor of the new Center Rog, a vibrant creative hub built on the site of the former ROG bicycle factory.
  • Kavarna SEM (map): A relaxed café next to the Slovene Ethnographic Museum, ideal for a coffee break before exploring the nearby Tabor district or the alternative cultural hub Metelkova.

Kids and Families

  • Hiša eksperimentov (map): A fun, hands-on science center with plenty of playful experiments.
  • Mala ulica (map): A family center with a play area for kids and various activities
  • Vlakec Urban: A small tourist train connecting the castle, major attractions, and the city center—a fun and easy way to explore the highlights without too much walking.
  • Ljubljana Zoo (map): Located in the green Tivoli–Rožnik hill area.
  • Hiša iluzij (map): A fun museum featuring optical illusions and tricks

Off the Beaten Path

  • Tivoli Park (map): The city’s largest park, perfect for a stroll or a run. If you enter via Cankarjeva Street, you can also pass the Opera House, the National Gallery, and the Museum of Modern Art.
  • Špica (map): A relaxing riverside spot loved by locals, ideal for a coffee by the water.
  • Krakovo & Eipprova Street (map): A charming neighborhood with cozy restaurants and a village-like vibe.

Events

  • Various Tours and Trips offered by the Ljubljana Tourism Centre
  • Various Events (many free of charge) taking place at the end of summer
  • Odprta kuhna (map): A vibrant outdoor food market at Pogačarjev trg, a 2-minute walk from the conference venue, open every sunny Friday until evening, featuring local and international street food.
  • Ljubljana Festival: Summer festival featuring classical music, opera, ballet, and cultural events at open-air and historic venues across the city.
  • Imago Sloveniae: A rich program of classical concerts, cultural performances, and art events across Ljubljana, many of them free of charge.

Medical Support

In case you need medical assistance during your stay in Ljubljana, the following services are available:

  • Public medical care: Emergency Unit, University Medical Centre Ljubljana (Bohoriceva 22a). For children, visit Pediatric Emergency Room, Community Health Centre (Metelkova 9).
  • Emergency dental services: Dental Service of the Ljubljana Community Health Centre (Metelkova 9).
  • Nearest private doctor: Dr. Nely (Kopitarjeva 4).
  • Rescue and emergency services: dial 112.
