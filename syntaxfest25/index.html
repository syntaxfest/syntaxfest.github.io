SyntaxFest 2025
Ljubljana, August 26–29

5 events for 1 Fest of Empirical Syntax

SyntaxFest is a biennial event that brings together a series of events focusing on topics such as empirical syntax, linguistic annotation, statistical language analysis, and natural language processing.

SyntaxFest 2025 will host five events under a unified submission process and program:

  • TLT: 23rd Workshop on Treebanks and Linguistic Theories
  • DepLing: 8th International Conference on Dependency Linguistics
  • UDW: 8th Universal Dependencies Workshop
  • IWPT: 18th International Conference on Parsing Technologies
  • QUASY: 3rd Workshop on Quantitative Syntax

In addition, SyntaxFest 2025 will be co-located with the UniDive 1st Shared Task on Morphosyntactic Parsing, organized by the UniDive COST Action CA21167 on 26 August 2025.

Important dates

Paper submission deadline 15 April 2025
Notification of acceptance 2 June 2025
Camera-ready papers 16 June 2025
Early bird registration June 2025
Conference dates 26 to 29 August 2025
