The 18th International Workshop on Treebanks and Linguistic Theories will take place, as one of the 4 events of the Syntax Fest 2019.

It will occur on the last week of August (Aug 26-30), in the center of Paris, in the "Grand amphitheatre du Monde anglophone" of the Sorbonne Nouvelle.

Important Dates

April 28, 2019 will be the deadline for submission of long papers and June 5, 2019 for short papers.
The submission and review process will be joint, but the 4 events will publish separate proceedings. The authors will have the possibility to choose to submit to one or several of the four events.
